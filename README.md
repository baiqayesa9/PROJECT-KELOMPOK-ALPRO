# E-Commerce Platform - PROJECT-KELOMPOK-ALPRO

## 📌 Project Overview
A complete e-commerce platform that allows users to browse and purchase a wide variety of products across multiple categories.

## 🛍️ Product Categories

### 1. **Electronics**
   - Computers & Laptops
   - Smartphones & Tablets
   - Accessories (chargers, cables, headphones)
   - Smart Home Devices

### 2. **Clothing**
   - Men's Fashion
   - Women's Fashion
   - Kids' Wear
   - Footwear & Accessories

### 3. **Food & Groceries**
   - Fresh Produce
   - Packaged Food
   - Beverages
   - Snacks & Confectionery

### 4. **Other Products**
   - Home & Furniture
   - Books & Media
   - Toys & Games
   - Beauty & Personal Care

## 🛒 How to Order Products

1. **Browse** - Select a category or search for products
2. **View Details** - Check product information and reviews
3. **Add to Cart** - Select quantity and add items
4. **Checkout** - Review cart and enter shipping details
5. **Payment** - Complete payment securely
6. **Confirmation** - Receive order confirmation and tracking number

## ✨ Features
- User-friendly interface
- Secure payment processing
- Real-time order tracking
- Customer reviews & ratings
- Fast shipping options
- 24/7 customer support
